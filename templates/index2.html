Data Used
for the Model

The data consists of the liver profiles of 583 patients from Andhra Pradesh, India in addition to 181 synthetically generated healthy profiles.

Description of
the Model

The model is a multilayer perceptron with 5 layers and 46 nodes in total. Many tactics, such as a heavy use of dropout, were implemented to prevent overfitting and improve accuracy.
